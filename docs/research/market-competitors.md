# Market and competitive research

Prepared 2026-07-01. Online made-to-measure apparel, German-speaking / Swiss focus. Prices are
advertised starting prices in each brand's currency and climb with fabric/options; market-size
and % figures are directional third-party estimates.

## The landscape

The category is large and growing (custom/made-to-measure apparel roughly USD 55-60bn 2024-25,
~9.5-11.7% CAGR). Three tiers online:

1. **Global volume** (Hockerty/Sumissura, Tailor Store, Modern Tailor, iTailor) - deep online
   config, Asia production, low starting prices, algorithm sizing, remake guarantees.
2. **Premium online specialists** (Proper Cloth, Suitsupply Custom, Son of a Tailor) - higher
   price, stronger brand, better fit systems, real return/refund guarantees.
3. **Swiss / DACH local tailors online** (Alferano, Sunita, PKZ MTM, Emanis, Huebscher) -
   showroom + in-person measuring, high price, "hybrid online" (configure/inspire online, measure
   in person).

Cautionary precedent: **Selfnation** (Swiss, online algorithm-fit jeans/chinos, ~15,000
customers) ceased own operations in 2020 and folded into Tailor Store - the pure-online Swiss
MTM-trousers model is buildable but hard to sustain on trousers alone.

Indicative prices: Hockerty shirt from ~$59, chinos ~$99; Tailor Store shirt ~$79; Proper Cloth
shirt from $125 (60-day money-back + 2 free remakes); Suitsupply Custom shirt $119; Swiss
in-person: Alferano CHF 179, PKZ CHF 159, Emanis CHF 180. Sizing is moving from self-measure
toward algorithm/smart-size (Proper Cloth Smart Sizes, Hockerty AI, Tailor Store data-sizing) and
at the edge phone body-scan (MTailor). (Hockerty CHF figures unverified - site blocked.)

## The gap CUTURA should own

Nobody cleanly occupies **"premium Swiss made-to-measure, self-serve online, calm brand, Swiss
QC."** Hockerty/Sumissura are Zurich-domiciled but read techy/global/value; the genuine Swiss
premium names are appointment/showroom businesses, not configure-yourself pay-online flows. The
in-between - **online convenience + Swiss trust + premium restraint** - is open.

## Customer barriers and trust signals

Barriers (in weight order): (1) **fit uncertainty** - the dominant objection (fit drives ~67% of
fashion returns); (2) **"can I return it"** - amplified because CH gives no automatic return and
custom goods are non-returnable, so the guarantee is the entire trust mechanism; (3) **fabric
feel unseen** - answered with mailed swatches (table stakes at premium); (4) **delivery time** -
2-3 wk (volume) to 4-6 wk (tailors) vs Swiss 24-48h norms; the risk is under-communicating, not
the length; (5) **price justification** - Swiss buyers rank quality #1 (~40% top value, ~21%
sustainability) and pay premiums when the price is visibly justified.

Trust signals that win: a plainly-worded fit guarantee shown prominently; a human backstop on fit
(photo/first-order review - Proper Cloth reviews before making); mailed swatches + honest fabric
info; transparent buffered lead times + order status; social proof (CUTURA lacks this at launch,
so lean on the guarantee + Swiss-QC story). **Swissness is a monetizable trust asset** (up to ~50%
uplift for luxury), but claims must stay honest (checked in CH, made in Vietnam; never imply
Swiss-made garments).

Swiss specifics: **TWINT is effectively mandatory** (86% of shops offer it, ~85.6% online use);
proper **DE/FR** (+ IT/EN) converts better; Swiss-Post-grade tracking; understated tone over hype.

## Prioritized service menu

**Launch now (highest leverage):** prominent fit guarantee; mailed fabric swatches (small fee
credited to first order, curated selection); saved measurements + one-click reorder; guided/smart
sizing with tape as fallback + a human size-confirmation on the first order (the founder approval
gate does double duty); TWINT + card; honest Swiss-QC / made-to-order traceability story; honest
buffered lead times + order status.

**Add soon:** gift cards/gifting; referral; a light lookbook/styling content; async measuring
help.

**Defer / later:** body-scan app; subscription/wardrobe-refresh; corporate/team/wedding orders
(good later margin, ops-heavy); full 3D configurator; languages beyond DE/FR at launch.

## Positioning recommendation

Own **"premium, calm, self-serve online made-to-measure with Swiss trust."** Core message
suggestion: _"Made to your measurements. Checked in Switzerland. Kept simple."_ Precision without
fuss; premium without hype; the guarantee removes the risk.

The 3-5 highest-leverage launch moves: (1) make the **fit guarantee the hero** (+ human review of
the first order before production); (2) frame sizing as **guided/smart**, not "get out a tape
measure"; (3) ship **TWINT + native DE/FR + honest lead times**; (4) tell an **honest Swiss-QC +
made-to-order** story (never imply Swiss-made); (5) build the **reorder loop from day one** (saved
measurements + one-click reorder + swatches). Consciously NOT at launch: 3D configurator,
body-scan, subscription, corporate/wedding, keyword-stuffed SEO copy.
