# CUTURA launch research dossier

Prepared 2026-07-01 as part of the pre-launch research campaign. These documents gather
diligent, web-sourced research to inform the launch decisions and the website content
(legal pages, fit guarantee, FAQ, measuring guide, pricing, positioning).

> **This is research, not legal or tax advice.** Every legal, tax, and data-protection item
> flagged "verify with lawyer / tax advisor" must be signed off by a qualified professional
> before it goes on the production site, consistent with the project rule that legal pages
> require lawyer sign-off. Sources are cited with access dates; nothing was invented, and
> genuine uncertainty is flagged in each document.

## Headline takeaways

- The app is built; the gap to launch is provisioning + real legal content + a few decisions.
- **Launch Switzerland-only first.** Liechtenstein is in the EEA, which pulls in GDPR, an EU
  representative, and an EU-style withdrawal right. Selling CH-only avoids all of that; add
  Liechtenstein later once the EU posture is handled.
- **Hard data-protection blocker:** no customer measurements may be sent to the Vietnam
  producer in live operation until a data-processing agreement + standard contractual clauses
  are in place, and only the minimum data should be sent.
- **VAT:** 8.1%, shown tax-inclusive; clothing is standard-rated; zero customs duty on textiles
  from Vietnam (industrial tariffs abolished 2024); import VAT is reclaimable once registered.
- **"Swiss made" is not allowed** (produced in Vietnam). Only truthful activity/company claims
  ("a Swiss brand, designed and quality-checked in Switzerland, produced in Vietnam").
- **Keep the fit guarantee** (remake-first, keep the original, money-back fallback) - it is at
  the generous end of the market and is the core trust lever; a strong measuring guide is what
  keeps it cheap.
- **TWINT is effectively mandatory** for a credible Swiss checkout.
